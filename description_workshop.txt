[h1]Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok: Weapon DLC[/h1]
[h3]An DLC Add-on to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3456517686] Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok[/url][/h3]
[b]This mod can be played on its own and doesn't require the main mod to run.[/b]

The Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok: Weapon DLC is a mod that aims to fill in the weak spots of progression with new, exciting items, as well as to be an suitable extension of the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=3142064272] Unofficial Calamity Bard & Healer[/url] mod.

It currently adds the following:
[list]
	[*] 6 Melee weapons
	[*] 3 Ranged weapons
	[*] 3 Mage weapons
	[*] 1 Summoner weapon
	[*] 2 Rogue/Thrower weapons
	[*] 18 Bard weapons
	[*] 10 Healer weapons
	[*] 2 Multi-class weapons
	[*] 2 accessories
[/list]

For more information on the weapons added by this mod, check out the official [url=https://terrariamods.wiki.gg/wiki/Infernal_Eclipse_of_Ragnarok/Weapons_(Weapons_DLC)] wiki![/url]

It is recommended to use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2619954303&searchtext=recipe] Recipe Browser[/url] to see how to obtain the new weapons.

This mod also has combatability with Consolaria & Secrets of the Shadows.

If you have any issues please report them in the [url=https://discord.gg/tc5nZdRvaU] Discord server[/url].
